About Milk + Honey

Milk + Honey is a music collective based out of Bellingham, WA. We're made up of producers, DJs, talent buyers, promoters, photographers, engineers, students, and more.

We have a passion for reading liner notes, discovering artists on the internet, exposing talent we believe is underrepresented (either in our market, or across the board), and throwing live shows that both attendees and acts won't soon forget.
